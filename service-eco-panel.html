What is EcoPanel?

EcoPanel is an innovative building system that revolutionizes construction efficiency and sustainability. Our panels consist of a fire-retardant polystyrene insulation core combined with a welded wire mesh frame featuring a diagonal wire truss for superior structural integrity.

Designed for rapid deployment and reliability, EcoPanel is suitable for any type of building project, from single-family homes to high-rise commercial structures. The system reduces construction time significantly while maintaining exceptional quality and durability.

EcoPanel System Components

Our EcoPanel system integrates carefully engineered components for optimal performance and durability.

Polystyrene Core

High-performance insulation core with fire-retardant additives offering:

  • Excellent thermal properties
  • Non-flammable composition
  • Superior compressive strength
  • Moisture resistant

Wire Mesh Frame

Welded wire mesh provides structural support with:

  • Diagonal wire truss reinforcement
  • Enhanced load-bearing capacity
  • Uniform stress distribution
  • Durability and reliability

System Integration

Complete building system featuring efficient integration:

  • Seamless wall construction
  • Floor and ceiling systems
  • Partition and facade solutions
  • Custom configurations available
