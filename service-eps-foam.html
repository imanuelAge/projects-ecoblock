What is EPS Foam?

Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is a rigid cellular plastic material that has been the world's most widely used insulation material since 1951. Our EPS foam products are engineered for superior performance, durability, and cost-effectiveness.

Available in sheets and blocks of various sizes and densities, our EPS products are the material of choice for architects, contractors, and manufacturers across multiple industries due to their exceptional properties and versatility.

Excellent Insulation

Superior thermal resistance provides excellent insulation for energy-efficient constructions.

Lightweight

Minimal weight makes handling, transportation, and installation fast and cost-effective.

Easy to Install

Can be easily cut, shaped, and positioned without special equipment or expertise.

Environmentally Friendly

Recyclable and sustainable, with minimal environmental impact when disposed properly.
