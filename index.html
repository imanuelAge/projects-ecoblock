Your Trusted Partner Since 2008

Our Services

We provide comprehensive construction solutions tailored to meet your specific needs. From concept to completion, our expert team ensures quality at every stage.

Expandable Polystyrene (EPS) Sheet and Blocks

Our EPS Foam (Sheets and Blocks), is the most widely used wall insulation, expansion joints, sound barrier, thermal insulation sources in the world since 1951 and comes in different sizes and densities. The sheets are also used in the packaging products for exportIts. Its low cost and versatile use makes it a material of choice for architects and contractors and in all construction applications.

Learn More

EcoPanel

Our sustainable ECOPANEL consists of a polystyrene insulation core that has fire retardant additives (non-flammable), and a welded wire mesh frame with a Diagonal Wire Truss. It is used for the construction of any type of building including residential, industrial, high-rise buildings, swimming pools, school projects, hospitals, industrial parks, etc.

Learn More

Architectural Shapes and Designs

Our premier brand which is the creative ARCHITECTURAL Shapes and Designs is also for a high-end finish on both commercial and residential buildings using expanded Polystyrene System (EPS) and coated with polymer cement and other chemicals to give it a concrete-like finish.
